My Musical projects

Ever since I've been a kid, I've had a need to scratch my musical itch. When I was a kid, I would play songs with my brother, with him playing "drums" we made out of cardboard boxes or old toys we had. As I grew, I started to go farther and farther in my musicianship, eventually joining a band and playing all around san francisco under the guidance of the San Francisco Rock Project. Nowadays, I make my own music, and you can check it out here!

So what even is my music, anyways?

My music is a blend of indie, bedroom pop, oldies, and 80s new wave/LA disco that I grew up with and love. I sing mostly about love, it's a crazy feeling and a great motivator to write! I also sing about the pressures of modern life, and really music is just a creative outlet for me. My interests lie in science for a long-term career, and I don't really desire to do anything with music because it just seems really draining to have to tour, make music on a schedule, etc. I don't work that way!
